COMPANY PROFILE

Established in 2005 by our visionary and enterprising founder Eng Francis .K. Mwatha, Airweb (EA) ltd is a company that deals with {HVAC-R} Heating Ventilation, Electrical, Air Conditioning & Refrigeration services with the aim of excelling in this industry. We are one of the leading providers of HVAC-R services in East Africa and we take pride in the fact that our customers have been availing of our services over the years and can vouch for our satisfactory services. As the providers of air conditioning and ventilation services we understand that our customers are our biggest asset and we aim to keep them happy and pleased at all times. This is why we have become a well-known and recognized service in our area and have been recommended as one of the best places to get professional HVAC-R services. For this we are ever so grateful to our client base, and our way of thanking them is by constantly improvising on our products and services and our customer care operations. In today's fast paced and dynamically evolving world it becomes essential that every business evolves and improvises to keep up with changing times. At Airweb (EA) ltd we do just that. Our policy of changing with times to suit the needs of an evolving society have held us in good stead thus far, and we intend to continue walking along the path of progress.
